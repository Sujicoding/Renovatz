Works Under Plumbing and Electrical

  • Plumbing
    1. Installation of plumbing fixtures: Installation of sinks, faucets, toilets, showers, bathtubs, and other plumbing fixtures.
    2. Pipe installation: Installation of water supply pipes, drainage pipes, and gas pipes.
    3. Repair and maintenance: Fixing leaks, repairing or replacing damaged pipes, unclogging drains, and maintaining plumbing systems.
    4. Hot water system installation: Installation of water heaters, including tankless water heaters, electric or gas-powered water heaters
    5. Plumbing system design: Planning and designing the plumbing layout for new constructions or renovations.
    6. Sewage system installation: Installation of sewage and septic systems for wastewater management.
    7. Water treatment system installation: Installation of water filtration or purification systems to improve water quality.
    8. Backflow prevention: Installation and maintenance of backflow prevention devices to ensure the integrity of the water supply.
  • Electrical
    1. Electrical wiring: Installation of electrical wiring systems for power distribution.
    2. Lighting installation: Installation of indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures, including recessed lights, chandeliers, wall sconces, and landscape lighting.
    3. Switches and outlets: Installation and replacement of switches, outlets, and dimmers.
    4. Circuit breaker panel installation: Installation or upgrade of electrical service panels and circuit breakers.
    5. Electrical system design: Designing electrical systems based on load requirements and safety regulations.
    6. Troubleshooting and repair: Identifying and resolving electrical faults, such as circuit shorts, power outages, and malfunctioning equipment.
    7. Safety inspections: Conducting electrical safety inspections to ensure compliance with electrical codes and regulations.
    8. Grounding and bonding: Installation of grounding systems and bonding to ensure electrical safety.
    9. Generator installation: Installation of backup generators for power outage situations.
    10. Energy-efficient upgrades: Installation of energy-efficient lighting, smart home systems, and energy management systems.
